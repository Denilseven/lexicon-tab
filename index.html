How do I use this?

The "name" of an option can be whatever you want. As for the link, you can grab the URL in the address bar for the site you just searched a word in and then replace that word with an all-caps "LEXICON"!

Examples

Dictionary:
https://www.dictionary.com/browse/LEXICON

Thesaurus:
https://www.thesaurus.com/browse/LEXICON

Urban Dict.:
https://www.urbandictionary.com/define.php?term=LEXICON
